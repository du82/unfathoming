Hello there,

I'm an aspiring generalist in the field of computer science with a focus on offensive cybersecurity. I care a lot about privacy and freedom, and I write (usually open source) software that helps restore and improve privacy.

This site, Unfathoming, is a capsule of my thoughts and ideas over time so that others may benefit from the things that float around in my head. In the event that I disappear, this site will serve as a reference as to who I am/was.

I recommend reading on a device with a large screen if you can. It gives you the ability to see the sidebar as well as sidenotes and other things that you might enjoy.
